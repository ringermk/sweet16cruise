Day 5 - Roatán, Honduras (Snorkel day)

Calm water, reef right off the beach, and a plan designed to be relaxed and low-stress.

Arrive: 7:00 AM Depart: 5:00 PM Back onboard: ~3:30–4:00

The plan (in one sentence)

Shared taxi to West Bay Beach → snorkel right from shore → lunch and shade → head back with plenty of buffer.

Why West Bay works

The reef starts very close to shore, the entry is sandy, and the water is usually calm — no boats or excursions required.

This is one of the easiest snorkel spots in the Caribbean.
