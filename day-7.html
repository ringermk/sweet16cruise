Packing Tonight (Important but Easy)

Tonight is when checked luggage goes out into the hallway. After that, you’ll only have what’s in your carry-on.

What goes in the hallway
Clothes you don’t need again, extra shoes, souvenirs, and anything you won’t need until tomorrow afternoon.
What stays with you
Pajamas, toothbrush, meds, tomorrow’s outfit, phone chargers, and anything you’d be sad to lose.
Timing matters
Luggage usually needs to be outside your cabin by late evening. We’ll double-check the exact time on the daily schedule.
