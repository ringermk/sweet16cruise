Spending Controls: No Surprise Bills

The cruise card they give you is a room key and a tap-to-buy wand. We’ll keep things fun for Jaekin and stress-free for Mom with simple guardrails.

Goal: zero bill anxiety Plan: block + fun money

TL;DR

Yes, the card can charge things
Mocktails, Starbucks, arcade, shops, photos, Wi-Fi upgrades — small stuff adds up fast.
Guest Services can restrict charging
We can disable purchases on Jaekin’s card while keeping room & ship access.
Smoothest setup
Block charging + add optional fun money.

Common Spending Traps

🍹 Drinks & treats
Specialty coffees, mocktails, smoothies, desserts.
🎮 Arcade
Tap → play → repeat → surprise total 😄
🛍️ Shops
Souvenirs, hoodies, snacks, shiny distractions.
📸 Photos & 📶 Wi-Fi
Classic “wait… that costs money?” moments.

How We Stay in Control

Best option (recommended)
Ask Guest Services to disable onboard charging for Jaekin’s card.
Optional teen freedom
Add a small fun money budget (example: $40–$75).
Golden cruise question
“Is this included?”

Embarkation Day Checklist

✔ Decide: block only OR block + fun money
✔ Visit Guest Services
✔ Request charging restriction
✔ Quick account check daily

Final Vibe

This isn’t about trust — cruise cards are just too easy. Guardrails = fun trip + peaceful Mom. ❤️
