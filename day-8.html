Today is the “hug the ship goodbye” day: Galveston → Wichita → Shawnee. It might feel a little sad — so we’re going to keep it gentle, organized, and full of snacks.

Theme: soft landing Carry-on: yes (luggage gets collected) Mission: home safe + comfy

The Plan (reverse Day 0)

We’ll get off the ship in the morning, regroup at the terminal, then start the long drive home.

Disembark guess (realistic)
Many cruises start letting guests off around 7:00–8:00 AM once customs clears, and you’re often asked to be out of your cabin around 8:00 AM. We’ll aim for: off the ship by ~9:00 AM with buffer.
Luggage reality
If we put checked luggage out the night before, we won’t see it again until the terminal. That means: carry-on only from dinner onward.
Arrival time vibe
This is a long drive. If we leave Galveston mid-morning, we’ll likely hit Wichita late evening, then Shawnee around midnight-ish (give or take stops, traffic, and snack emergencies).
